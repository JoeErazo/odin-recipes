Black Bean Soup

This recipe for black bean soup is fast, easy, and sure to delight your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, divided
  • 1 (14 ounce) can chicken broth
  • 2 cups cubed cooked ham
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon cumin
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Stir in onion, bell pepper, carrot, and garlic; cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Mix in 1/2 of the black beans, then add chicken broth.
  2. In a blender, purée remaining beans until smooth; stir into the pot and bring soup to a boil. Reduce the heat to low. Mix in ham, salt, cumin, and pepper; simmer for 20 minutes. Garnish with Cheddar cheese and sour cream to serve.
